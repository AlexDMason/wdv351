What to be aware of

      Certain videos may be age restricted and require the user to log in.
      An accumalation of cache and cookies on the website can lead to videos slowing down or not working.
      After embedding the video on to the page, you should still make it look presentable.

Features

Most of the video player feature you can find on the Youtube site itself will work when being embedded on a website. Fullscreen, captions, settings, device casting, subscribe to channel, watch later, share, recommendations, and being able to visit the Youtube site itself.
